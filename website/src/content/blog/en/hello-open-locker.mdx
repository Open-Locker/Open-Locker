Open Locker is an open source project providing **software and hardware blueprints** for public lockers to store and/or share items.

## Why we're building this

In Hameln-Pyrmont, public lockers are already being used to lend out items such as laptops and VR headsets. Open Locker aims to improve the user experience and make the system easier to adapt.

## How you can get involved

- Weekly meetup every Tuesday at **7:30 PM** (CET) on Discord
- Issues and pull requests on GitHub
